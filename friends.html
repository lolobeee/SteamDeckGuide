6. Friends and Chat

As on the desktop version of Steam, you have access to your Steam Friends on the Steam Deck.

You can see what they are currently and have been playing. You can also chat with your friends just like on the desktop version!

This section goes over how to interact with your Steam Friends!

6.1 Access the Friends and Chat Menu

To access the Friends and Chat Menu:

  1. Press the Steam button.
  2. Select Friends and Chat.


Figure 6.1: Friends and Chat located in the Steam Menu

6.2 Chat

To chat with your Steam Friends:

  1. Go to the Friends and Chat menu (Steam > Friends and Chat)
  2. Find the friend you want to chat with within your friends list
  3. Start typing your message in the chatbox!

Figure 6.2: Steam Chat with a Steam Friend.

To start a chat with several friends:

  1. Go to the Group Chat tab.
  2. Select Create Group Chat.
  3. Create a Chat Name in the dialogue box.
  4. Invite several friends to the chat from your friends list.
  5. Start typing your message in the chatbox!

Figure 6.3: Creating a new Group Chat.

6.3 Add a Friend

To add someone as a Friend:

  1. Go to the Friends and Chat menu (Steam > Friends and Chat)
  2. Select the Add Friend button (an icon of a person with a plus sign.)
  3. Enter in their Friend Code into the dialogue box.

Figure 6.4: The Add Friend button.

Figure 6.5: Adding a Friend via their Friend Code.
