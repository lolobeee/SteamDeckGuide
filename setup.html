2. Out of the Box Setup

Now that you have opened the box of your new Steam Deck, let's go over how to get it setup!

Before you can turn on your Steam Deck, you'll need to plug it in to charge for a short time. Use the provided power supply and charging cable.

Once it has charged for a few minutes:

  1. Press the Power button until the screen turns on.
  2. When prompted, select your preferred language from the options listed on the screen.

Note:

To move through menus, you can use the Left Thumbstick, Touchscreen, D-Pad, or Right Trackpad.

To select an option, press A.

To go back an option, press B.
