8. Downloads

8.1 Accessing the Downloads Menu

The Downloads Menu allows you to view your Steam Deck's latest downloads. This is where you will find the progress of any games you are downloading. You can also view any updates to your games here.


Figure 8.1: The location of the Downloads Menu within the Steam Menu.

8.2 Downloads Menu

The Downloads Menu is organized into two categories:

  • Up Next: upcoming downloads, like updates for games
  • Completed: completed downloads

Within the Downloads menu, you can click the A Button on any of the titles to be taken to that game's Page in your Steam Library.


Figure 8.2: The Downloads Menu.

Navigating over a game and pressing the Menu Button allows you to view the Options menu. Depending on the type of download (i.e. upcoming or completed), you can do several things including looking at its patch notes or viewing the game's Page within the Steam Library.
