8. Media

8.1 Accessing the Media Menu

To access the Media Menu, press the Steam button and select Media.


Figure 8.1: Media within the Steam Menu.

8.2 Taking Screenshots

To take a screenshot, press the Steam button and the R1 button.

You can press both of the buttons simultaneously, or press the Steam button first and hold it before pressing the R1 button.

Your screenshots are saved within the Media Menu.

8.3 Recording Gameplay

Also stored in the Media Menu are Game Recordings.

To take video of your gameplay, go to Settings in the Steam Menu. Then, go to Game Recordings.

In this menu, you have the option to change your recording mode:

  • Recording Off: Steam will not record your gameplay
  • Record in Background: Steam will automatically record your gameplay as you start playing
  • Record Manually: Steam will record video only after you press Steam + A

Figure 8.2: The Game Recording menu.

For more information, please go to Steam Store: Game Recording.

8.4 Managing Media

You can view your screenshots and game recordings in the Media Menu.

To share your screenshots and recordings, first select the screenshot or recording.

Then, select the sharing method of your choosing:

  • Share to Steam: this allows you to post your media to your Steam Account, either publicly or privately.
  • Copy to Clipboard: this allows you to copy/paste your screenshot within the Steam Deck.
  • Send to Phone: this allows you to send the media to your Steam Mobile App.
  • Share to a Chat: this allows you to share your screenshot to a Chat with a Steam Friend.

Figure 8.3: The Sharing Media Menu.
